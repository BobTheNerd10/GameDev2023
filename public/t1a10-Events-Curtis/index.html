T1a10-Events-Curtis


There's lots of cool things you can do with events!

Here are some of them...

onclick

Onclick activates whenever you click an element, try clicking the button below!

onmouseover

Onmouseover activates whenever you hover your mouse over an element, try petting the cat with your cursor!

onscroll

Onscroll triggers whenever you scroll the viewport! Try scrolling now!

onerror

On error triggers whenever an element that loads something has an error! Thankfully, that will never happen with MY coding, so you'll never get to see this light turn on! >:D

onkeydown and onkeyup

Onkeydown and onkeyup trigger whenever you press and release a key on your keyboard!
Unfortunately, you need to use addEventListener on the window to get this one to work :[

Try pressing the F button!
